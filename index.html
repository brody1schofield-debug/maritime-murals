About the Artist

🎨

Payton Schofield

I'm a muralist and painter based in New Brunswick, Canada. I create custom murals and artwork that bring spaces to life — from cozy children's bedrooms to large-scale commercial walls.

Every project starts with your vision. Whether you have a detailed concept in mind or just a colour palette and a feeling, I'll work with you to create something truly unique for your space.
