About the Artist

Where Walls Become Canvases

Hey — I'm Payton, the artist behind Maritime Murals. I've always believed that every blank wall is just an opportunity waiting to explode with colour.

From vibrant bedroom transformations to eye-catching commercial installations that stop people in their tracks, I bring bold energy and a fearless approach to colour to every project. Each mural starts with your vision and ends with something that makes your space unmistakably alive.

Based in New Brunswick with roots across the Maritimes, I bring east coast grit, creativity, and a whole lot of paint to every wall I touch.

— Payton Schofield
